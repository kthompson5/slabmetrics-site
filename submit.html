Packing Guide

  • Each card in a penny sleeve inside a Card Saver
  • Team bag > cardboard sandwich > bubble wrap
  • Use a sturdy box; avoid plain envelopes
  • Include your packing slip and order ID

Disclaimer: Suggested Grades are opinions for transparency. We take normal care but cannot guarantee against postal damage. For high-value items, choose insured return shipping.
